Our Mission

At Euclid, we believe in forming partnerships with our clients — not holding one-sided conversations. Whether you’re in the beginning stages of retirement planning or already retired, our mission is to provide the highest levels of service to help our clients enjoy their lives in retirement rather than worry about their money.

To best serve our customers, Bountiful Investment Management holds and represents the same value system; they help serve the Euclid community. While Euclid specializes in retirement income planning, Bountiful serves our clients by specializing in investment planning. This partnership allows Euclid to provide service that supports our mission of providing a holistic approach for our client's retirement needs.

The Four Pillars of Retirement

Are you missing one of the key pillars of a successful retirement?
We believe you should address these 4 areas:

Income
planning

Unlike the weekly salary you had when you were employed, most retirement income gets paid monthly. We can help design a stable, consistent income to meet your unique needs.

Tax
planning Strategies

Without proper planning, taxes can take a big bite out of your retirement income. We’ll help organize your sources of income to help you avoid paying more than your fair share in taxes.

Healthcare
planning

In addition to understanding your healthcare options, we’ll help you plan and budget for future potential healthcare costs.

Legacy
planning

Legacy planning is so much more than just the houses, cars or money you leave behind. We’ll help you organize your assets and prized possessions to help ensure they are distributed according to your values and wishes after you’ve passed away.
